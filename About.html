About her

KyeongAh Kang is a passionate graphic designer who is also a master of visionary thought. KyeongAh Kang has studied at George Brown college for graphic design for 3 years and graduated the school in 2020. She hopes she gets involved in the user experience field but also open-minded to make her career in various fields. This website will be a great opportunity to get to know her with her marvelous works.

Brief history

She was born in YongIn, South Korea. She studied digital media at MyongJi university in Seoul. She graduated from MyongJi university in 2017. She got involved in graphic design at George Brown college in 2018 and graduated from GBC in 2020.
